Reloado Auth is the only two-factor authenticator you will ever need.

Built for privacy. Designed for trust. Reloado Auth protects your accounts with end-to-end encrypted cloud backup: Meaning only you can ever access your codes. Not even we can. Zero compromise.


END-TO-END ENCRYPTED BACKUP, SYNC & WEB ACCESS

Your 2FA tokens are encrypted in your vault and can be synced seamlessly across all your devices. Access your codes from any browser with full end-to-end encryption intact. Whether you're on your phone, tablet, or a desktop, Reloado Auth has you covered. Your keys. Your data. Always.


WORKS OFFLINE — NO ACCOUNT REQUIRED

Generate TOTP and HOTP codes entirely offline. No internet connection needed, no account required. Use Reloado Auth locally for as long as you want (cloud sync is always optional).


BIOMETRIC UNLOCK

Your vault stays locked behind biometric authentication. Only your fingerprint or face can open it. Fast, seamless, and secure.


INSTANT CODE CAPTURE

Scan a QR code or enter accounts manually in seconds. Tap to copy your code instantly: No more squinting at digits and typing them out.


UNIVERSAL COMPATIBILITY & MULTILINGUAL SUPPORT

Reloado Auth supports both TOTP and HOTP standards and works with every major service — Google, GitHub, Microsoft, and any RFC 6238 or RFC 4226 compatible platform. Available on Android, iOS, and Web. Fully localized in English and German.
